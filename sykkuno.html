Sykkuno Facts

  • He has a dog named Bimbus whose pictures he often uploads to his Instagram account.
  • As of September 2020, he had more than 600k followers on his Twitch account.
  • He started his popular YouTube channel on September 20, 2011. However, one of the earliest posts on his channel was uploaded on January 28, 2012. It was titled Corki – Revvin’ Back Up! – Full Gameplay/Commentary.
  • As of 2020, some of the most popular videos on his YouTube channel were THE PERFECT GAME! 1v6 – Among Us, Disguised Toast & friends, craziest vent kill I’ve ever done in Among Us, I clutched this impostor game for us!, this stealth kill method never failed me as impostor, and Karthus – KARTHUS IS CRAZEEE! – Full Gameplay/Commentary.
  • He does not follow any kind of strict diet routine and instead relishes the taste of fast food like burgers.
  • In early December 2020, after getting 620,000 followers in the previous 30 days, he had become the fastest-growing streamer on Twitch.
  • In January 2021, he revealed that he had moved to Las Vegas.
  • In February 2021, Sykkuno was asked if he would join the GTA RP and the NoPixel server. He answered that most of his friends weren’t playing GTA. Since he enjoys playing with friends, hence, he would play that game which his friends would want him to play.
  • In March 2021, he had crossed 3 million Twitch followers.
  • In March 2021, he revealed that his name “Sykkuno” doesn’t have a hidden meaning behind it and he randomly thought of it.
  • In April 2021, along with Corpse Husband and Disguised Toast, he sent an 11-year-old Among Us fan ‘Happy Birthday’ wishes which made him cry with happiness.
  • In July 2021, Sykkuno revealed that, unlike many other streamers such as Ludwig Ahgren and Hasan ‘HasanAbi’ Piker, he had no interest in sub-athons and sub-goals.
  • In September 2021, he tweeted that he wanted to get unverified on Twitter. Corpse Husband and Imane ‘Pokimane’ Anys publicly supported this decision of him.
  • In November 2021, Sykkuno revealed that according to him, xQc was the #1 streamer on all of Twitch.
  • Every year, May 1, is National Sykkuno Day.
  • On May 1, 2022, he delivered an emotional speech on Twitch during the ‘National Sykkuno Day’ stream. Because of that, many people wondered if he was leaving Twitch for YouTube. Just the next day, on May 2, 2022, he revealed that he was exclusively moving to YouTube Gaming for streaming.
