Lewis Hamilton, undeniably one of the most successful drivers in Formula One, has reached an insane level of fame, earned a corresponding amount of money, and is living the high life. The world-class racer is an unparalleled property dealer of the top-notch level. Over the years, the seven-time F1 champ has accumulated a portfolio of opulent abodes spanning across the world’s fanciest capitals. With an estimated net worth of $300 to $500 million, it is to no one's surprise that he has one of the most expensive real estate portfolios ever, owning some high-end properties spread worldwide. Let's take a look at each of these astounding Lewis Hamilton houses.

The Luxurious Home in Kensington

Kensington, West London, is home to one of the most luxurious Lewis Hamilton residences. It houses an enormous kitchen and distinct dining area for entertaining guests and arranging parties. The palatial house is a four-story mansion with six luxurious bedrooms, an impressive washroom, and a 200 feet posh green garden that accommodates a delightful summer house. Additionally, there are four reception quarters for casual companies.

The property's prior masters are believed to be financier Ben Goldsmith and Burberry chief designer Christopher Bailey according to The Sun. Samuel Johns was the leading architect of the house in 1860. This sprawling mansion is estimated to be worth $25 million.

The Glamorous Geneva Apartment

Lewis Hamilton is also the proud owner of a beguiling Geneva apartment situated above a six-story structure in Luins village, Nyon. Several renowned racing drivers are also rumored to own apartments here, such as Felipe Massa, Michael Schumacher, and Fernando Alonso. The charming Geneva home includes three voluminous bedrooms with an unbelievable view of Geneva lake. This property is a storehouse of serenity and contentment. The apartment houses a warm and welcoming living area, a well-equipped kitchen, an expansive washroom that has ample space to accommodate a small gathering.

One of the marvelous aspects of this residence is that it houses a small gym with all the state-of-the-art facilities and equipment for comprehensive and flawless fitness training. The home is placed in a plush locality in Geneva and has a glorious ambiance. As Essentially Sports reports, the F1 sports champion, Hamilton, is often spotted hanging around this home spending quality time, which is not surprising as it is an absolute joy of a home.

The Princely Monaco Condo

Being home to one of the most illustrious and esteemed Formula One racing stages, almost all racers have a luxurious residence in Monaco to unwind from the tracks. Numerous former and current F1 drivers reside in Monaco, and therefore it's clear Hamilton wouldn't miss out on owning a house based in the principality.

It is believed that the British sports star spends his time at the gym and the sauna when he resides in one of the world’s smallest countries in the world. His exceptional apartment is in the elite Fontvieille district, with a minor population of only a little above 3000 people. Hamilton’s exclusive Monaco home houses a 300 square feet sun terrace, a steam room, and four bathrooms.

According to Reports, the F1 champ splurged an estimate of $15 million to get his hands on this fantastic property. It has been reported that Hamilton chose a more modest home compared to the other properties in the area as they were on the market for double the price. Hamilton made an intelligent decision to go with the wonderful deal he got on his current residence.

The Expensive Big Apple Penthouse

Hamilton's New York penthouse was listed for $57 million in 2019, according to Forbes. This 8,900 square foot property at 443 Greenwich in Tribeca is housed in the exact building as other celebrity residents such as Jake Gyllenhaal, Meg Ryan, Bella Hadid, The Weeknd, Jessica Biel, and Justin Timberlake. Tribeca has been ranked as the most costly neighborhood in Manhattan consecutively for the past few years, largely due to the rush of super-prime condos that are on the market for tens of millions of dollars.

Hamilton has a kinship to America, particularly the east coast, where he has held two residences in New York. In 2019, he splurged $43.4 million on a Manhattan penthouse partially owned by NFL legend Tom Brady and his wife supermodel Gisele Bundchen, as GQ reports. The sprawling home of 6,547 square feet comprised a spacious outdoor arena, overlooked the Hudson River, and housed a fitness center, squash court, swimming pool, wine cellar, fitness center, and library. The Brit champion’s neighbors included Austin Powers star Mike Myers and Oscar-winning actress Jennifer Lawrence.

Being one of Britain's wealthiest sports persons ever, Lewis Hamilton has more than enough money to splurge on expensive and stunning properties spread around the globe. From a $57 million penthouse in New York to a $25 million London mansion and a Monaco condo, the 36-year-old world champion surely knows how to live a posh life.
