Who Is Suki Waterhouse?

Suki Waterhouse is a model, actor, and singer

Per IMDb, Waterhouse is an English actress, model, and entrepreneur. She started modeling when she was 16 years old after being discovered in a London pub. After being the face of Burberry Brit Rhythm Fragrance, she started walking the runway for major fashion houses like Balenciaga and Vivienne Westwood.

Waterhouse has also graced the cover of several prominent magazines. Some of them include:

  • Vogue
  • Elle
  • L'Officiel
  • Marie Claire

In 2017, Waterhouse was named the "Mercier Muse" of makeup brand Laura Mercier.

How Did Robert Pattinson And Suki Waterhouse Meet?

Robert Pattinson and Suki Waterhouse met via friends in common

The two were first linked to each other in July 2018. They were seen kissing on the streets of London after watching Mamma Mia! Here We Go Again at Electric Cinema in Notting Hill.

"Along the way, they were very close and cuddled and kissed many times," a source told E!. "Robert was very affectionate to her and had his arm around her the whole way and Suki reciprocated by holding his hand and kissing it. They were very loved up and close. Robert stroked Suki's hair and gave her another loving kiss."

The couple had already been dating for months at that time. Another insider said that they share "a love of music" and that they "are both really cool, nice and normal people and fun-loving." It also seems like they agreed on keeping their relationship under wraps. Pattinson was previously in a high-profile relationship with his Twilight co-star Kristen Stewart. Then at some point, his relationship with ex-fiancé FKA Twigs, which was filled with controversy as a result of racist comments the couple received when together.

It was not until 2019, that the Tenet actor eventually made his first statement about Waterhouse.

When asked about their relationship, he answered: "Do I have to [talk about her]?"

According to Pattinson, it's "extremely rude" to ask about people's relationships.

"If you let people in, it devalues what love is. If a stranger on the street asked you about your relationship, you'd think it extremely rude. If you put up a wall it ends up better," Pattinson told The Sunday Times. "I can't understand how someone can walk down the street holding hands, and it's the same as when I do it and a hundred people are taking your photo. The line between when you're performing and when you're not will eventually get washed away and you'll go completely mad."

What's Robert Pattinson And Suki Waterhouse's Relationship Like?

Robert Pattinson and Suki Waterhouse like to keep details of their personal life quiet

In a recent interview with GQ, Pattinson recalled a time when Waterhouse kept entertaining an obsessive DC fan who was talking to him non-stop. "And I'm sitting there facing the other direction, and my girlfriend just keeps continuing the conversation with him," said the actor of the fan who was there to fix his broiler.

According to Pattinson, the model extended the chat just to tease him. "And I'm looking at her like: 'Shut the f--- up,'" he continued. "'Why are you doing this to me?' She was very entertaining. Just talking to an obsessive fan."

Since that time, Pattinson and Waterhouse have progressed with their relationship. Waterhouse announced that she was expecting the couple's first child together in November 2023. Then, the two got engaged in December 2023.

What Pattinson and Waterhouse plan to do as far as their wedding is concerned is unknown. But 2024 will be a big year for the two as that is when their bundle of joy will be arriving.
