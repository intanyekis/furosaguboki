At first glance, the cast members of A&E's hit series Duck Dynasty may not seem like they have a ton of cash ... but looks can be deceiving.

The Robertsons are now an incredibly wealthy family, and some Duck Dynasty net worths more than tripled since the end of the show's run and today.

Updated, February 2024: On February 19th, the magazine Woman's World published an exclusive interview with Duck Dynasty "matriarch" Kay Robertson, on the subject of her marriage to Phil Robertson, and a film – The Blind – depicting the couple's real-life battle to save their marriage, and their "tumultuous early years" together.

In the article, Kay Robertson credits her faith for rebuilding the couple's connection. When asked what advice she had for "women whose marriage has hit a rough spot," Kay said:

"I tell them to pray and pray and pray and also to know that God made our marriages to last forever, so He doesn’t want it to end. We have to work and fight for our marriage. That was my favorite words in the movie: 'Fight for your marriage!'"

Whether they made their money from associated businesses like Duck Commander or from their time on the television series, the folks who appeared on Duck Dynasty walked away from the project much richer.

Let's take a look at the main cast members of Duck Dynasty's net worths, as well as the show's couples and their shared fortunes.

Rebecca Robertson

Current Estimated Net Worth: $100,000

Rebecca Robertson joined the family at the age of 16, when she emigrated from Taiwan to the United States as an exchange student.

It wasn't long before she became their foster child, and not long after that, the Robertsons officially adopted her.

Rebecca is an aspiring fashion designer who hasn't made many appearances on the television series since joining the clan, but she did show up in the series' fifth season.

Additionally, she co-edited the family's fashion blog, "Duck & Dressing."

Rebecca Robertson lives in Los Angeles, and has an estimated net worth of $100,000.

John Luke & Mary Kate Robertson

Current Estimated Net Worth (Combined): $800,000

John Luke and Mary Kate Robertson have a combined net worth of around $800,000 as of August 2023.

John Luke was best known on the series for being one of the few male cast members without a long beard, though he has since stopped shaving like the rest of the family.

John Luke is the son of Willie and Korie Robertson, and the second-oldest following Rebecca.

He first stepped into the spotlight at the age of 14 but has branched off and written two books, a children's book titled Be Your Own Commander, and Young And Beardless, which was published in 2016.

She was the first runner-up on season 19 of Dancing With The Stars and has tried her luck at professional acting.

Justin Martin

Current Estimated Net Worth: $700,000

While Justin Martin isn't a member of the Robertson family, he's been a part of the family business since 2008.

When the series debuted in 2012, Justin was one of the company's employees.

Justin's work centers around physically building the Duck Commander, and the company's other impressive duck calls.

He's taken some flack from the other cast members for his large size, even earning the nickname "Friar Tuck" from Phil, but he takes it in stride.

His net worth was estimated to be approximately $700,000 as of late 2023.

John Godwin

Current Estimated Net Worth: $1,500,000

John Godwin is another employee of the company and star of the series who isn't related to the Robertson family, but that hasn't held him back in the least.

Godwin first met Phil Robertson when he was a teenager participating in a duck call competition. Phil was one of the judges (and even tuned up his call).

Godwin ended up working for Duck Commander in 2002 and remained with the company well into the creation of Duck Dynasty.

Jep & Jessica Robertson

Current Estimated Net Worth (Combined): $8,000,000

Jep Robertson and his wife Jessica are sitting pretty on a mountain of cash thanks to their time on the show.

Jep is the youngest of Phil's children, and like his siblings, he has been an important part of both the family business and the television series since its inception.

Jep, whose real name is Jules Jeptha, also specializes in videography. He films and edits all of the DVDs of the Robertson's family duck hunts, which are sold on the company's website.

Si & Christine Robertson

Current Estimated Net Worth: $8,000,000

Silas "Si" Robertson is Phil's brother and the uncle to his children.

Si fought in Vietnam, carrying a Tupperware cup his mother sent him, a habit he continued through to the airing of Duck Dynasty.

Si worked at Duck Commander until he retired in the series' eleventh season. His specialty was the creation of each and every reed that went into the company's many duck calls.

Si married Christine (who only appeared on the series finale, having declined to appear in any other episodes).

Si Robertson's net worth is estimated at around $8 million.

Jase & Missy Robertson

Current Estimated Net Worth (Combined): $10,000,000

Jase Robertson is the second child of Phil and Kay Robertson, who works as the COO of Duck Commander.

He's a professional duck hunter who loves to go fishing and hunting whenever the opportunity strikes.

He's one of the company's best innovators whose responsibilities include creating new and innovative duck calls, as well as fine-tuning the company's regular stock.

One of his greatest inventions was the "Triple Threat" duck call, which uses three reeds instead of the normal two used in other calls.

Jase is married to Kay Robertson, and they have three children, Reed, Cole, and Mia.

As of late 2023, Jase Robertson's net worth was estimated at around $10 million.

Willie & Korie Robertson

Current Estimated Net Worth (Combined): $45,000,000

Willie Robertson works as the CEO of the company and was one of the most active members of the family in terms of the show.

Willie is a businessman, hunter, outdoorsman, actor, and author.

Phil & Kay Robertson

Current Estimated Net Worth (Combined): $10,000,000

Phil Robertson is the patriarch of the Robertson family, and is married to Kay Robertson.

In 1973, Phil developed the Duck Commander, a duck call so successful that it became the gold standard duck whistle among professional hunters.

Phil, along with his wife Kay and their four sons, worked together to turn the success of the Duck Commander into a multi-million dollar business.

From that business, the family grew their name and reputation until they managed to land their own television series on A&E, bringing their brand of homespun wealth to a worldwide audience.

As of late 2023, Phil Robertson's net worth was estimated to be approximately $10 million.
