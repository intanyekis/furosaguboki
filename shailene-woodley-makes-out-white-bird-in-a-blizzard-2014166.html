Shailene Woodley Makes Out -- A Lot -- in White Bird in a Blizzard

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  207 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Geez, Shai, get a room already! Though her Fault in Our Stars character was a shy virgin, Shailene Woodley is about to explore a whole new type of girl in her upcoming indie film White Bird in a Blizzard. 

Playing Kat Connor—a young girl experimenting with her newfound sexuality after the mysterious disappearance of her strict mother—Woodley, 22, really goes to town on costar Shiloh Fernandez, who plays her boyfriend Phil. 

In a special excerpt from the film, Kat tries everything from making out on top of a car to skinny dipping with her equally eager beau. The thriller, which is set to come out later this year, also stars Eva Green as Woodley’s missing mother and Christopher Meloni as her distant father.

White Bird in the Blizzard is the next movie in a series of successes for the former Secret Life of the American Teenager star. After landing the coveted role of Tris Prior in the Divergent series, the Golden Globe nominee also scored the lead role in the adaptation of John Green’s bestselling novel The Fault in Our Stars.

White Bird in the Blizzard will be released on iTunes on Sept. 25, and in theaters on Oct. 24.
