Roberto Cavalli

Birthname Roberto Cavalli Birthdate November 15, 1940 Birthplace Florence, Italy Height 180 lbs or 82 kg Weight 180 lbs (82 kg) Profession Fashion Designer Net worth $500,000,000 Source of Wealth Fashion Designing Nationality Italy Country Italy Marital Status Married (Eva Duringer) Children 5 (Tommaso, Christiana, Robert, Rachele, Daniele)

Best noted for his creations with detailed exotic prints, Roberto Cavalli is a well known Italian fashion designer from Florence with an estimated net worth of $500 million in 2012.

He is famous in creating an extraordinary printing technique on leather which earned him a patent right for it. Debuting in Paris, some famous fashion houses in the city, like Pierre Cardin and Hermes, have benefited from his work of art. It was in 1982 when his first boutique was opened. In 1994, his major creation, the sand blasted jeans were introduced into the market, which was later sold in more than 50 countries worldwide. Subsequently, he started launching his own fashion lines like Just Cavalli, which later featured eyewear, accessories, women’s wear, men’s wear, underwear, perfume, and beachwear. There were also other lines like shoes, eyewear, underwear, perfumes, watches and Angels & Devils Children Collection.

In 1997, he was chosen to be one of the judges in the famous Miss Universe Pageant, where he met his future wife, Austria’s representative Eva Duringer, who finished second. His child Daniele Cavalli is also a famous fashion designer. His marriage to Duringer resulted to three children named Daniele, Rachele and Robert. On the other hand, he has two children from previous marriage. They were named Christiana and Tommaso.
