Gwyneth Paltrows Exes, Ranked By Net Worth

 Posted on May 24, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1043 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Gwyneth Paltrow has been a big name in the entertainment industry since her breakout role in the Steven Spielberg 1991 film Hook. She is known for her leading role as Viola De Lesseps in the 1998 film Shakespeare in Love. More recently, Paltrow has been a part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe as Pepper Potts, Tony Stark’s love interest throughout the franchise. Paltrow is also a business owner with Goop, a wellness brand that sells a variety of products from candles, sex aid, and vitamins.

Somehow, in between her insane schedule as an actress and businesswoman, Paltrow has had time to create a long list of ex-boyfriends. Paltrow has dated many celebrities, and she has been married to two famous men. Let’s get into everyone Gwyneth Paltrow has dated and how much they are worth.

8 How Much Is Donovan Leitch Jr. Worth?

Donovan Leitch Jr. was the first celebrity Gwyneth Paltrow was linked to in a romantic way. The two celebrities briefly dated in 1993, and Leitch currently has a net worth of $8 million. He primarily worked in the entertainment industry in the 1990s, but has made some appearances in more recent years.

Leitch is best known for his work in the film The Blob as Paul Taylor. The film was released in 1988. He has had some guest spots in television shows, including Grey’s Anatomy and Sex and the City. Leitch is also a singer and has been a part of two bands, Camp Freddy and Nancy Boy.

7 How Much Is Robert Sean Leonard Worth?

Robert Sean Leonard and Gwyneth Paltrow dated for about a year in the 1990s. Leonard is a stage actor and television actor with a net worth of $10 million. He is best known for his work in the hit television show House.

Leonard played the role of Dr. James Wilson on House, which ran from 2004 to 2012. Directly following the end of House, just as star Hugh Laurie moved on, Leonard did too. He returned to theater acting. He played the role of Paul Verrall in the play Born Yesterday. Leonard also has a Tony Award for Best Performance by a Featured Actor in a Play for his role in The Invention of Love in 2001.

6 How Much Is Chris Heinz Worth?

This question is more complicated than fans may realize. Chris Heinz dated Gwyneth Paltrow very briefly in 2001 after her break up from Ben Affleck. Currently, Heinz has a net worth of about $10 million, but that is subject to change.

Heinz, as his name suggests, is the heir to the Heinz Ketchup business. The ketchup brand is estimated to be worth $750 million. His mother, Teresa Heinz Kerry, is currently the next heir. Heinz exact inheritance will be determined by his mother’s stake in the company.

5 How Much Is Brad Falchuk Worth?

Brad Falchuk is the current husband of actress and businesswoman Gwyneth Paltrow. They met in 2014, but kept their relationship a secret for a year before going public. The couple announced their engagement in November 2017 and were married the following year. Falchuk loves talking about living with Paltrow.

Falchuk is a television writer, which is how the two met. Paltrow had a guest role on Falchuk’s show Glee.Falchuk has an impressive list of television shows he has created and written, which includes not only Glee but also Pose, Scream Queens, and American Horror Story. Falchuk has a net worth of $30 million.

4 How much Is Luke Wilson Worth?

The Heinz Ketchup heir was not the only celebrity Gwyneth Paltrow dated in 2001. She was also linked to actor Luke Wilson that year, and the two dated for about a year before calling it quits. They met on set of the film The Royal Tenenbaums.

Luke Wilson has amassed his net worth of $50 million through his acting. He is known for his roles in Legally Blonde, Charlie’s Angels, and Anchorman. He joined the cast of Zombieland: Double Tap as the character Albuquerque. Wilson recently opened up about his nerves regarding turning 50 years old.

3 How Much Is Chris Martin Worth?

Chris Martin was the first husband of Gwyneth Paltrow. The two met in 2002 backstage of one of Martin’s concerts, just weeks after the passing of Paltrow’s father. They got married in 2003 and have two children together, Apple and Moses. In 2014, Paltrow announced their “conscious uncoupling,” but the two maintain a good relationship for the purpose of co-parenting.

Martin is the lead singer of the popular band Coldplay. The band found worldwide success in 2000 with the song “Yellow” and have been a hit ever since. Martin has seven Grammy Awards and a net worth of $130 million.

2 How Much Is Ben Affleck Worth?

Ben Affleck and Gwyneth Paltrow dated on and off from 1997 to 2000. During that time, they starred in two films together. In both Shakespeare in Love and Bounce, they actually played love interests.

Affleck is a highly successful actor and filmmaker with a net worth of $150 million. His list of accomplishments includes Good Will Hunting, Gone Girl, and Argo. Affleck was previously married to Jennifer Garner, and is now married to Jennifer Lopez. Affleck and Lopez previously dated and were engaged in 2003, and fans love that they found their way back to each other.

1 How Much Is Brad Pitt Worth?

Not surprisingly, Brad Pitt is the richest man Gwyneth Paltrow has ever dated. He has a net worth of $300 million. The two met while filming the movie Se7en, which also starred Morgan Freeman, and dated from 1994 to 1997. They got engaged in 1996, but chose to call off the wedding plans just seven months later.

In an interview with Howard Stern in 2015, Paltrow spoke about Pitt and their former relationship. “I was such a kid. I was 22 when we met,” she said of the actor. “You can’t make that decision when you’re 22 years old. I wasn’t ready, and he was too good for me.”
