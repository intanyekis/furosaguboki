Professional Life

Paul Scheuring took his first try at being a television show writer after working on 36K in 2000 and A Man Apart in 2003. Then he wrote Prison Break, which he pitched to Fox but was rejected owing to its unusual plot.

Then, he made Lost, and after twenty months, it was released by FOX. For the Lost series, he gained worldwide fame and won the 2006 People's Choice Award, and was nominated for Best Drama Television series at the Golden Globe Awards.

Scheuring has also started filming AR2, a drama he co-created with Thomas Schlamme, the director and executive producer of The West Wing. He's also the producer of Masterwork, a Fox and 20th TV drama pilot script, and the Inferno-produced film The Experiment.

In October 2019, Paul was named executive producer and new showrunner for Season 3 of Tom Clancy's Jack Ryan franchise.
