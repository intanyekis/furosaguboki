The pair spend time in each other's companies with their significant others and even go on double dates. Bradley Cooper and Taylor Swift move in the same Hollywood circle, and his girlfriend, Gigi Hadid, is Swift's longtime friend. Why Did Bradley Cooper Reject Taylor Swift?

Long before dating Joe Alwyn, Matt Healy, Calvin Harris and Travis Kelce, Taylor Swift set her sights on Bradley Cooper. At the time, a 23-year-old Swift had recently gotten out of a relationship and was interested in the "American Sniper" actor. According to RadarOnline, Swift enlisted Jennifer Lawrence to set up a date between her and Cooper. At the time, there were rumors that Lawrence was involved with the actor due to their onscreen chemistry.

This was untrue because Jenifer Lawrence wasted no time playing a matchmaker between the stars. However, nothing came of it. According to a Bradley Cooper insider, the actor was not interested in Taylor Swift. The insider revealed, "First of all, her reputation precedes her. Bradley is very wary of dating someone who is a bit of a serial dater like Taylor." The insider added:

"He thinks she's far too young for him and wants to date someone his own age, not 16 years younger, because he's ready for something serious."

The source continued, revealing that the situation was "a little awkward" for Bradley Cooper when Jennifer Lawrence told him about Taylor Swift. They also made it known that the actor politely declined because he knew Lawrence and the "Bad Blood" singer were friends. After describing Swift's romantic interest in Cooper as "ambitious," the source noted that she would have to "fixate on someone else."
