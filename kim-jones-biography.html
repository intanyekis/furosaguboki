Who is Kim Jones?

Kim Jones is an American reporter best known for her work as a clubhouse reporter in YES Network ‘New York Yankees games and also for her work on NFL Network and WFAN radio.

This New York-based reporter is not only eminent for her work as a sports reporter but also for her excellent body figure of 32-26-35 inches.

Age, Parents, Siblings, Family, Ethnicity, Nationality

Kim Jones was born on 7 October 1969, in Pennsylvania, USA. Her nationality is American and her ethnicity is North American.

Born Kimberly Alicia Jones in Dallastown of Pennsylvania, United States of America was brought up in Pennsylvania with her family.

As a child carrying a dream to be a reporter and a sportscaster, she was involved in various sports and mass speaking classes from a very early age in her life.

While participating in a Basketball competition, Kim Jones used to be on the sidelines which developed her interest in reporting as well.

Education, School/College University

For the purpose of fulfilling her dream to be a sportswoman and a reporter, she joined Penn State University and majored in Sports.

Along with sports, Kim Jones also minored in Journalism making her capable of living her dream in the coming future. After her graduation, she started to work as a reporter in various live castings.

Kim Jones: Professional Life, Career, Net Worth

Kim Jones’s career as a reporter was initiated just after her graduation.

Jones was a regular reporter making usual appearances on the Penn State Radio Program ‘Let’s Talk Penn State’ in which, she also aired her own segment ‘Keeping Up with the Jones’.

After such appearances in The Penn State Radio program, she went to Newark, New Jersey to be a reporter for ‘New York Giants’ and a columnist for ‘The Star-Legend newspaper.

As a supporter of the New York Yankees, Kim Jones joined YES Network to be a reporter and a sportscaster for ‘New York Yankees which was followed by her work with ‘NFL Network’ and ‘WFAN radio.

Currently, Kim Jones works for ‘NFL Network’. As of 2023, Kim Jones has an estimated net worth of £9 million.
