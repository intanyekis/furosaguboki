Dolly Parton Expands $650 Million Net Worth With New Line Of Wine

Highlights

  • Dolly Parton's countless business investments, including amusement parks, contribute to her massive $650 million net worth.
  • The iconic singer is expanding her fortunes with a line of personalized wines, showing her savvy in new ventures.
  • Parton's notable success in music, theme parks, films, and more solidifies her as a shrewd businesswoman beyond her legendary music career.

Dolly Parton has a reported net worth of $650 million, but it’s not all thanks to her legendary music career. The singer has made a variety of smart business investments over the years, including amusement parks, attributing to her high net worth.

Now, it appears Parton plans to expand her fortune even more through a line of personalized wines. According to reports, the singer has filed a trademark in American as well as the U.K. to establish her wine brand.

Dolly Parton makes her riches outside the limelight too.

“Dolly is always looking at new ways to reach her audience and thought her own wine was a swell idea,” a source told The Sun. “She isn’t a huge drinker herself but she likes to have something special when she does fancy a tipple.”

“She has been involved in developing the wine and is excited for it to hit the shelves,” they continued.

Dolly has admitted she rarely drinks alcohol, so her new business venture may come as a surprise. Jennifer Lopez recently endorsed an alcohol brand, despite admitting to rarely drinking, and it wasn’t received well by fans, who noted the hypocrisy. But so far, Dolly’s new project hasn’t received the same criticism.

Parton's impressive net worth is a testament to her multifaceted career and savvy business ventures. Beyond her music career, Parton has diversified her portfolio with various investments. She owns the Dollywood theme park in Tennessee, which has become a major tourist attraction.

Additionally, her stake in Sandollar Productions has seen success with hit films like Father of the Bride and Buffy the Vampire Slayer.

Parton's foray into the restaurant business includes partnerships with franchises like Dolly Parton's Stampede Dinner Attraction and various Dollywood eateries. Moreover, her branding extends to Dolly Records and Dolly Parton's Imagination Library, a literacy program that distributes free books to children.

More recently, Dolly found success upon launching her own clothing and accessory line for dogs , which debuted last year under the apt name Doggy Parton.

Through strategic investments and entrepreneurial endeavors, Parton's net worth has flourished, cementing her status as not only a music icon but also a shrewd businesswoman. Now, with her burgeoning wine line, Parton’s net worth only stands to grow – so long as fans are okay with her not drinking her own products.
