Kenya Moore's Daughter Brooklyn Wears a Pink Printed Swimsuit as She Walks Barefoot on a Beach

 Posted on June 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  403 words  |  Aldo Pusey

"The Real Housewives of Atlanta" star Kenya Moore's two-year-old daughter, Brooklyn, recently melted hearts with the most beautiful shot of her playing barefoot on the beach.

"The Real Housewives of Atlanta's" Kenya Moore is the luckiest mom around! In a recent picture shared on Instagram, fans got to see how adorable her daughter, Brooklyn, is. The caption of the cute picture read:

"Beach bum??"

Kenya Moore on Andy Cohen's "Watch What Happens Live!" on December 15, 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

The adorable snapshot captured Brooklyn looking like an absolute angel as she walked on the sandy white beach, with the beautiful blue ocean spreading out vastly behind her.

Brooklyn was dressed in a fabulous printed pink bathing suit that had a halter style neckline without any sleeves. Interested in getting the full experience of being at the beach, Brooklyn was barefoot and had a bag of chips in hand.

Brooklyn's followers could not resist how adorable she looked in her beachwear, and they all trooped to the comments with several heart emojis as they doted on her. Brooklyn is certainly the cutest beach bum around!

This happened amid speculations that Moore and Daly's marriage was experiencing troubles.

The exceptional Brooklyn has not been around for a long time, but she has very quickly become a favorite among her parents' fans and social media followers. Her parents also share several photos of their little one with the world very frequently.

Moore and her husband, Marc Daly, welcomed her in 2018. Last November, they celebrated her 2nd birthday in grand style. Moore proudly took to Instagram to share photos of the farm-themed party they threw for her. She wrote:

"Wishing everyone love and happiness. Thank you @freddyoart for capturing this moment. ??❤️ #2yearsold."

The birthday girl was dressed in the most fabulous pink overalls with a white long-sleeved shirt as she posed with her smiling parents. She completed the outfit with some sensational matching pink boots and had her hair up in a messy bun.

This happened amid speculations that Moore and Daly's marriage was experiencing troubles. Back in May, the couple openly considered having a second child, but now, the status of their relationship remains unclear.

Despite what could be unfavorable differences, the parents are devoted to making their daughter the happiest little girl. Judging from her fab beach picture, it seems that both father and mother are doing a pretty good job.
