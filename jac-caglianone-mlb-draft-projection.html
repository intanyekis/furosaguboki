Jac Caglianones MLB Draft Projection: Where Will He Go?

 Posted on June 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  572 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Jac Caglianone, aka “Jactani,” from Florida, is one of the country’s most exciting college baseball two-way prospects. With his strong pitching and smashes, Jac Caglianone draws attention at the plate and on the mound. 

With his comparisons to Los Angeles star Shohei Ohtani, the Florida Gators star has turned heads toward him throughout the tournament. It is too early to predict that the Tampa native has a future as a pitcher or a hitter based on the skill and power he showcases on both sides of the ball. 

With an amazing skillset and a bright future, Jac Caglianone is expected to be a top-10 pick in the draft of MLB in 2024. Here’s what you need to know about Jac Caglianone’s MLB draft projection. 

MLB Draft Eligibility for Jac Caglianone

Jac will be eligible to enter the MLB Draft 2024 as an undergraduate at Florida in 2023. As per the draft criteria, players at four-year colleges can enter the pool after three seasons or after the age of 21. Just before the resuming of the 2024 college season, Jac will turn 21, and he’ll be eligible for the next year’s draft. 

D1 Baseball has given him the no. 11 rank prospect for 2024 entering this season, but he has risen based on his performance. 

Where Will Jac Caglianone Go in MLB Draft 2024?


Where will Jac Caglianone go in MLB Draft 2024? Well, this thought is also wondering fans along with the teams. Let’s see which teams are interested in him:

For a two-way player, the team New York Mets are scouting for exciting prospects for the upcoming MLB Draft. With the need for first place baseman, they are looking for Caglianone to fill the role, and also they have a record for developing excellent pitchers who will also match with him. 

Another big name is San Francisco Giants, who can draft Jac for the season. With the history of nurturing and drafting college stars, Caglianone surely be on their list. They also require a first baseman, and Jac is the best option. 

With his all-rounder ability and amazing skills, there is no second thought that Jac will be a hot contender for the MLB Draft 2024. Caglianone has all the potential to be a star in the MLB League and will be a player to watch out for the 2024 season.

Jac Caglianone Stats in 2023

In 2023, Jac was an NCBWA Dick Howser Trophy finalist, achieving an NCBWA District 3 Player of the Year and a Golden Spikes Award. His variety of pitching and power ability makes him a very intriguing contender. In 2022, Jac was recovering from his elbow surgery didn’t make him eligible to pitch for the Gators, but he was a designated hitter in the late season.

Standing tall at 6 feet 5 inches and weighing 215 pounds, Jac has all the desired physical attributes for a top-tier pitcher. With 31 home runs and 84 RBIs, Caglianone hit .336, having a breakout season in 2023. With 81 strikeouts in 69 innings, Jac also pitched to a 4.14 ERA. Jac’s fastball also touches 90 mph consistently. 

His all-round performances earned him a nomination for the prestigious John Olerud Award, which was presented to the best two-way player in college baseball. Jac could go as high as the top five in the draft, and the team that will draft him gains a player with a lot of potential.
