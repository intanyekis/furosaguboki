Niharika Chouksey, who plays the title character in the new TV show “Faltu,” said that she has a lot in common with her character because both of them are brave, daring, and fearless.
She tells us: “Faltu and I have a lot in common because we are both outgoing and quick to speak our minds. She is sure of herself and says what she thinks. Most importantly, Faltu and Niharika both have a lot of hopes and dreams. I have a lot of feelings about the character.”

“I won’t say it isn’t hard, but it’s more of a responsibility because I play so many girls who have or are going through this hell. I hope I won’t let them down and that I’ll be able to give them hope and let them know they’re not alone.”

“Faltu” is about how people in rural areas treat their girl children and how they sometimes give them names like “Faltu” because they think the next child will be a boy. Niharika also plays the role of a country girl whose family calls her “Faltu” because they think she is useless because she is a girl.
When asked what Niharika thinks is the show’s USP and why people in cities will connect with it, she told IANS, “The USP of the show is definitely our story, which is so real and genuine, and that’s why people will connect with it because there are so many little girls who have to go through this hell and bury their dreams because they are considered unwanted and useless.”

Even though Niharika plays the main character on the show, she and Aakash Ahuja, who plays the male lead, have a sweet chemistry. His character, Ayaan, is an important part of Faltu’s life, and he wants to help her in some way, but society puts limits on him.
The “Yeh Rishta Kya Kehlata Hai” actress said this about how well she got along with Aakash: “We have a strong friendship, and that shows on screen. He is great at what he does and very helpful, and we both try to improvise and make scenes feel more real. Faltu’s life is very affected by who he is as a person. He is the one who shows her the way and keeps her safe. Faltu’s life changes for the better when she finds a mentor and coach in Ayaan.”
