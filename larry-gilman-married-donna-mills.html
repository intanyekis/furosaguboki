Is Larry Gilman Married to Donna Mills?

The world does not only remember Larry Gilman as a multifaced entertainer and actor but also as the long-term partner of famous actress Donna Mills. Because the couple has spent over 2 decades together, the question of whether they are married often comes up. Well, the 73-year-old Gilman has been dating Donna, 83, since 2001 but have decided to stay true and committed to each other without necessarily getting married. 

There is a lot about their relationship, how they met, and why they are not married in this article. Read through to find out.

All About Gilman’s Longterm Relationship with Donna Mills and Why They Are Not Married

Larry has been dating Knots Landing actress, Donna since 2001. They met shortly after Mills moved into her Brentwood home which she calls her “forever home”.

Gilman is about 10 years younger than Mills but the age difference does not seem to matter.

For all we know, the couple’s relationship has been blissful and they have a lot in common. Gilman reveals on his page that he and Mills have a penchant for wines, travel, theater, movies, and adventure, and in their relationship, they have a knack for growing together.

As part of their commitment and love for wine, Gilman gifted his partner a vineyard. At the time, Mills revealed that they intended to start their own wine.

Now, looking at the couple and their great relationship, the question that often comes up is why they are not married. The reason is not farfetched from their decision not to combine finances by marrying is much cleaner and much better.

Larry Gilman is the Second Man Mills Has Had a Lasting Relationship with

Long before Gilman started dating The World’s Oldest Living Bridesmaid actress, Mills had a relationship of over 2 decades with Richard Holland, an advertising executive and ex-husband of singer Chaka Khan.

The relationship started in 1979 and was on and off for 20 years. According to a report about her love life by Amomama.com, her relationship with Holland was a turmoil. A part of the public thought that he was a golddigger but then, Mills never cared for anyone before him as much as she cared for him.

While they were together, she helped in raising Holland’s child with Khan, Damian.

In another aspect of Larry Gilman’s partner’s relationship, she has been linked to John Gavin, Clint Eastwood, Michael Dean Shelton, and Winston Rekert.

Does the Couple Have any Children?

Gilman and Mills do not have any children together. Nonetheless, in 1994 at the age of 54 Donna Mills adopted a newborn baby girl, Chloe Mills. The girl was born on September 12 1994 and is 29 years old.

She is a model and shares a close relationship with her mother who often posts her on social media. Chloe also makes a number of public appearances with her mother.

Apart from her mother, Chloe also shares a close relationship with Gilman. He has been in her life since his love affair with Mills began in 2001. He revealed on his website that he is raising her along with his long-term partner.

The couple are also animal lovers and share dogs together.

Away from Larry Gilman’s Relationship with Donna, Here is What to Know About Him

Larry Gilman was born in New York City, New York, on April 3, 1950. He is 73 years old and has reached fame as an actor and producer.

In his acting career, he has featured in numerous movies and series dating from the 1970s. Gilman has worked both on Broadway and Off-Broadway. He has explored regional theater.

In 2015, he was the co-producer of the This Video Will Make You… episode in season 2 of the Whole Day series.

More into his career in the movie industry, Gilman has served as an assistant in Dark Harvest (2016) and City Boy (1992) films and series respectively.

In the quest to give more to his career, he has explored being a director. He has directed over 35 plays, radio recordings, and co-directed 2 movies. This aspect of his career would lead him to win an award for directing The Zoo Story with Woody Harrelson.

Despite his achievement in the movie industry, Mills’ partner has found ways to explore other interests. He is a farmer and owner of Mandeville Vineyards.

He has put in a lot of work towards making wine. With his grapes, he has created wines and hopes to make them even bigger and better.
