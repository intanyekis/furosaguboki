John Green is an American author, podcaster, YouTube content creator, and philanthropist who is best known for releasing books like The Fault in Our Stars, Let It Snow: Three Holiday Romances, Paper Towns, Will Grayson, Will Grayson, Looking for Alaska, This Star Won’t Go Out: The Life and Words of Esther Grace Earl, and The Anthropocene Reviewed: Essays on a Human-Centered Planet. He has more than 50 million copies in print worldwide and has been honored with numerous awards such as the Edgar Allan Poe Award, Los Angeles Times Book Prize, Webby Award, American Library Association Award, Goodreads Choice Award, and many more. He has launched several podcasts such as Dear Hank & John, The Anthropocene Reviewed, and Harry Potter and the Sacred Text.

Education

John Green first went to the Lake Highland Preparatory School in Orlando.

He then went to Indian Springs School which is located outside of Birmingham, Alabama. This school served as an inspiration for his setting of the book ‘Looking for Alaska’ in later years.

John Green attended and graduated from Kenyon College in 2000. He graduated with a double major in English and Religion Studies.

He had also enrolled at the University of Chicago Divinity School but, he never really attended the school.

    Best Known For

    John Green is best known as a writer of famous novels which won different awards in recent years. The three most known books that Green has written are – Looking for Alaska (2005), Paper Towns (2008), and The Fault in Our Stars (2012).

    Apart from writing, John Green is also known for being the launcher of VlogBrothers channel in 2007 on YouTube.

    First Book

    John Green’s first book is Looking for Alaska which was published by Dutton Children’s Book in the year 2005. The book was recognized as the “best book written for teens, based entirely on its literary merit.” It received the Michael L. Printz Award by the American Library Association in 2005.

    First Film Adaptation

    John Green’s fifth book The Fault in Our Stars has been adapted into a film by the same name and was released in the US on June 6, 2014.

    First TV Show

    John Green has appeared as ‘The John Green’ on the TV Series PogieJoe in 2013 and 2014 in a total of two episodes.

    First Film Appearance

    John Green was seen on the big screen in the movie The Fault in Our Stars (2014) as one gentleman at the airport.

    Personal Trainer

    John Green does not believe in exercising to remain fit. He is naturally healthy and has a slim body. His take on fitness and the excitement associated with it are captured in a hilarious YouTube video known as ‘Fitness for Nerds.’ He also tweeted once “Are there any personal trainers who can do the running and weightlifting for you, or is that not a thing”? This shows that the author has not taken exercising in his routine till date.

    However, the writer has a peculiar habit of walking while writing. He has a treadmill desk in his room. Whenever he faces a “writer’s block,” he paces to get inspiration.
